Why I chose these colors:

My goal in choosing these colors was to appeal to a more natural color pallete due to the tourism pull of the area. Black and White are going to be the main font colors based upon what background they appear upon. Actual color assignments will occur once more of the assignment is fleshed out, but I am planning one of the blues to make up the header/nav bar and the greens for background/main body.
