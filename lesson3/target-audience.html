Target Audience:

The primary target audience for this website will be local buissness owners. These individuals will likly hail from a variety of back grounds and ages, although they are likly middle age and above. The key aspect they share are buissness related problems.

Personas

Example 1:
This individual has been a buissness owner for some time before locating the website. They are probably most interested in assistance with marketing and lobbying efforts. The times they access the site will vary at multiple times, but computers and tablets are foreseen to be primary devices of contact.

Example 2:
This individual is a newer buissness owner. Due to this they would likly benefit more from networking with more established buissnesses as well educational rescources. The rest of the profile probably matches the first example, but this individual is more likly to fall into a younger demographic.

Scenarios

  • An individual is having difficulty marketing due to the expense. Using our service they are able to locate individuals with similar problems and can work together for a more cost efficient solution.
  • An older buissness owner is concerned about a municpal law about to be passed, but he is able to use the Chamber's lobbying efforts to look out for his intrests.
  • The owner of a small resteraunt thinks holding a special event at the height of tourist season could help bring in customers. They want to use our service to reach out to fellow buissness in the industry to see if they want to collaborate.
  • The coordinators of the Chamber want to host an event to help raise awareness of buissness opprotunities in the local economy. They are able to utilize newsletters to advise members as well as post the event information on the website for interested parties.
