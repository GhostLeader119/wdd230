United Valley Chamber


Welcome to Star Valley!

We are a group of towns on the Wyoming-Idaho border, just outside of Jackson Hole and Teton National Park. The approximate population of the area is 5215.


Snake River

The Snake River flows through Jackson Hole, goes down Snake River Canyon and empties in the Palisade Reservoir. This provides many opprotunites for both white water rafting and boating with several businesses that specialize in such activities in the area.


Winter Fun

The valley recieves several feet of snow during the winter leading to plenty of adventures for winter sports enthusiasts. There are a multitude of paths available for snowmobilers through the moauntain landscape providing ample challenges while many open spaces exist for novices to the sport.


Animal Life

The rural and remote nature of the Valley combined with its proximity to a few of America's natural parks makes it an excellent place to view wildlife. This is true whether you are camping or enjoying your backyard.


Camping

As mentioned and shown previously, there is alot of natural beauty in the surrounding area. As such, there is no shortage of interesting campaing spots.
